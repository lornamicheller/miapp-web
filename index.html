About MiApp

MiApp’s vision is to facilitate a new normal for healthcare and wellness by offering easy access to medical and wellness services through online appointment management and Remote Check-in technology. Our mission is to use technology to transform and improve the human experience between patients and their healthcare and wellness providers.

Features

Directory

Patients find MiApp doctors or wellness providers by name, region, specialty, or date of availability through the MiApp mobile directory.

Online Appointments

Patients schedule, confirm, cancel, or reschedule appointments directly from their mobile devices.

Remote Check-In

Many medical providers rely on paper sign-in sheets and waitlists at their physical offices. MiApp allows patients to check into their appointments from mobile devices through Remote Check-in. Remote Check-in holds patients’ places on the doctors and wellness providers’ virtual waitlists and notifies patients of their estimated wait time.

Intelligent Notifications

Patients receive timely, automatic notifications reminding them of their appointments and allowing them to confirm, cancel, or reschedule. On appointment day, notifications will update patients on their estimated wait time.

Going Green

MiApp substitutes the use of paper sign-in sheets
and waitlists with intelligent technology to promote more
environmentally friendly and ecologically responsible decisions.

MiApp's Benefits to Healthcare and Wellness Providers

Improves Communication Between Healthcare and Wellness Providers and Their Patients

Allows healthcare and wellness providers to efficiently inform patients of delays or conflicts affecting their appointments through mobile notifications

Improves patients’ attendance through consistent notifications leading up to the time of their appointments

Reduces patients’ lateness and no-show rates by sending reminders to patients and allowing patients to reschedule, if necessary, from their mobile devices

Alleviates the Consequences of High Phone Call Volumes

Reduces busy phone lines, long hold times, and missed phone calls that adversely affect medical practices by decreasing phone call dependency for matters of scheduling, confirming, canceling, or rescheduling appointments

Decreases stress related to high phone call volumes

Improves productivity of medical and wellness personnel by freeing up time from phone calls and allowing staff members to focus on other pressing issues

Improves the Office Environment

Eases the burden on doctors, wellness providers, and administrative staff of attending to full waiting rooms of anxious patients irritated by long wait times

Promotes Patient Health

Accommodates patients with chronic illnesses or conditions that inhibit them from sitting for extended periods of time in waiting room settings

Offers Advertising Opportunities

Provides healthcare and wellness providers the opportunity to advertise their practices through the MiApp Directory
